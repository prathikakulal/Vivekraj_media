About

Vivek Raj Poojary, a name synonymous with innovation and success, has carved a niche for himself in both the business and political arenas. Today, he is embarking on a groundbreaking venture that promises to revolutionize the agriculture industry: Indoor high-quality saffron farming using hydroponics. This ambitious project is set to redefine the standards of saffron cultivation, ensuring unmatched quality and consistent yield.

Trailblazing Success in Business
Vivek's illustrious career began in 2004 with a major iron ore excavation contract in Karnataka, marking his meteoric rise in business. His exceptional achievements earned him the Rajiv Gandhi Award in 2004 and the Indira Gandhi Award in 2005. In 2007, he was honored by the India Thai Chamber of Commerce, and in 2010, the World Economic Forum recognized him as the world's youngest CEO on the Global Growth Company platform at Davos, Switzerland.

Global Expansion and Influence
Vivek's expertise led to an invitation from Zimbabwe's Deputy Prime Minister in 2012 to develop iron ore mines, and in 2014, he joined the World Economic Forum under the Confederation of Indian Industries, alongside industry titans like Mukesh Ambani and Anand Mahindra. In 2016, he ventured into farming, becoming one of India's largest spice growers and traders. His innovative approach extended to the U.S., where he established a significant agricultural presence. Vivek's vision of indoor farming for saffron and medicinal plants promises to eliminate crop losses and guarantee 100% revenue.

Excellence in International Trade
Under Vivek's leadership, his company has expanded its trading portfolio to include organic fruits and vegetables sourced from around the world, with quality being the main agenda and principle. Contract farming initiatives in Spain, Africa, and various Asian countries have further solidified his global influence. The company trades in the best quality fruits, vegetables, meat, seafood, and cheese sourced from traditional makers worldwide, ensuring premium standards and customer satisfaction. Notably, the company is involved in salmon and tuna fishing and sources the best poultry meat from top suppliers, maintaining a commitment to excellence. Vivek's meticulous approach to trading ensures that only the finest products reach consumers. His company's network spans across continents, trading 35 different minerals from 72 countries and over 190 varieties of vegetables and fruits worldwide. This vast and diverse network not only guarantees quality but also fosters international collaboration, bringing the best of global produce to the market. Vivek's dedication to quality and sustainability in trading is setting new benchmarks in the industry.

Innovating the Future of Farming
Vivek's latest venture focuses on hydroponic cultivation of high-quality saffron indoors, ensuring optimal growth conditions and superior quality. Set to be among the largest indoor farming facilities by 2026, this project aims to revolutionize the spice industry. With the investments diversified in stocks, trading of minerals, and agricultural products trading, Vivek is well-prepared to lead this initiative. His company currently trades 35 different minerals from 72 countries and 190-plus varieties of vegetables and fruits worldwide. This extensive network and experience in global trade provide a solid foundation for the success of his indoor saffron farming venture. Farming in the USA and expanding indoor farming of spices, saffron, and medicinal plants is a key focus for Vivek. By 2027, he aims to be a major player in the indoor farming of medicinal, spices, and exotic fruits and vegetables in the USA. His vision includes converting current outdoor farming practices to reliable indoor farming, where everything from weather to growth is under control, leading to a 300% increase in profit. This forward-thinking approach is the future of farming, and Vivek seeks larger joint ventures to achieve this ambitious goal. Vivek's investment in hydroponic technology represents a leap towards sustainable agriculture. By controlling environmental factors, he ensures optimal growth conditions for saffron and other high-value crops. This method not only maximizes yield but also conserves water and reduces the need for pesticides, making it an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional farming. The potential to scale this model globally could transform agricultural practices and food security worldwide.

Journey from Humble Beginnings
Vivek Raj Poojary's journey to global recognition is a true rags-to-riches story. Coming from a humble background, he faced numerous challenges but remained undeterred. His hard work, determination, and innovative mindset propelled him from local success to international acclaim. Vivek's story is an inspiration to countless young entrepreneurs who aspire to make a mark on the world stage.

Commitment to Philanthropy
Beyond his business acumen, Vivek is dedicated to community upliftment. He supports farmers with advanced techniques, provides education for their children, and creates employment opportunities for locals through his business ventures. His charitable initiatives include organizing healthcare camps for underprivileged communities, improving farmers' yields and livelihoods, and opening educational opportunities for children to break the cycle of poverty. During the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, Vivek's philanthropic efforts were particularly impactful. He distributed 25,000 kits to feed families of four for 20 days, provided thousands of chappals for laborers, covered lakhs of medical bills, and supplied thousands of COVID kits and medicines to laborers and their families in Mangalore and Chikmangalur. His initiatives also included buying vegetables from farmers to support them during the pandemic, ensuring their livelihoods were protected despite the risks involved. Vivek's empathy and proactive support for the community have been exemplary. His efforts to improve the lives of farmers have made a significant difference. By introducing advanced farming techniques and providing necessary resources, he has helped many farmers achieve better yields and improve their livelihoods. His charitable endeavors have touched thousands of lives, providing hope and opportunity where it was needed most.

Passion for Antiques and Cultural Heritage
In addition to his business and philanthropic pursuits, Vivek is a passionate collector of fine antiques. He is the single biggest collector of LLADRO limited edition pieces in the world and owns an extensive collection of fine and expensive Montblanc pens. His collection includes the finest Persian mats dating back to the Persian era and rare artifacts procured through various auction houses around the world. Vivek plans to start his own auction house to hunt and find the finest antiques, which collectors can preserve and cherish. This venture is set to be ready in three months. Vivek's collection also features the finest Indian art and paintings, along with exquisite woodwork made from sandalwood, rosewood, and teak. He boasts collections of LLADRO, ERTE, RENE LALIQUE, MONTBLANC, and WATERFORD pieces, with the rarest limited edition items. His passion for preserving cultural heritage through these artifacts is not just a hobby but a mission to keep the rich history alive for future generations.

Leadership and Political Impact
In recognition of his numerous achievements and significant contributions to business and society, Vivek Raj Poojary was appointed as the District Incharge of Chikkamagaluru, a picturesque hill station in Karnataka renowned for its coffee and agricultural heritage, by a prominent national political party. Supported by DK Shivakumar, whom Vivek regards as his leader, he was entrusted with the coordinator in-charge role for Chikkamagaluru. Vivek's strategic and financial capabilities were instrumental in ensuring victory in the assembly elections in the district. His efforts extended beyond his immediate responsibilities, encompassing other areas as well. Using their international data collection and strategy, they contributed significantly to the Congress party's success, engaging in door-to-door campaigns through WhatsApp and SMS. Vivek's leadership under DK Shivakumar and the Gandhi family is marked by his strategic vision, relentless pursuit of excellence, and unwavering commitment to ethical practices. His ability to foresee trends and adapt to changing market dynamics has positioned him as a forward-thinking leader in the global business community. As he continues to break new ground and inspire others, Vivek Raj Poojary's legacy is one of innovation, impact, and integrity.
